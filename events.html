Alumni Talk

Alumni from various sectors are invited to the campus to interact with the IITG student community. This event is purposefully designed to cater to the needs of the students and answer their queries about career options, placements, pursuing interests, academia, etc. Students are invited to participate in the events and discussions and make the best use of the opportunity to learn and experience the perspective of their alumnus/alumna.


Webinar

In this age and time of the fast-paced world, it sometimes becomes difficult for alumni or speakers to take out time from their busy schedule and come down to the college to give seminars, or talks, to the students of the campus. To solve this problem, SAIL organizes webinars, wherein the speaker can connect with the students right from his home or office. It's user-friendly for the speaker and the students too, as the students can listen to the seminars right from their room. Webinars are topic-specific, and all the webinars are stored on the SAIL youtube channel.



Graduation Tea Party

GTP, or the Graduation Tea Party is the official farewell event of the graduating batch. Every year, SAIL organises the Graduation Tea Party in the month of April. The event stands as a benchmark to the start of a new beginning while emotions and cheers make this event a lifetime memory.


International Students' Day

IITG hosts a vast number of foreign interns and exchange students throughout the year and encourages them to spend summer/academic semesters at IITG. Every year, SAIL organizes the International Students' day where the students from outside the sub-continent participate in varied events and exhibit their talents. Celebrating diversity, this event holds a unique position in the history of IIT Guwahati.

Finalis Resonare

Finalis Resonare. The final note in the modal melody that the four years of college life has been. It is an event that is exclusively for the final yearites, where everyone come together for one last time, to create everlasting memories, and to celebrate and cherish all that makes college life what it is. With cultural performances, award ceremonies, roasts, and man other events, it is sure to be a fun-filled night.


Student Alumni Meet

Student Alumni Meet, or SAM, are organized in various cities, with an aim to increase the connectivity between the alumni, students and the campus. Multiple chapters of SAM have been conducted, including in the cities of Singapore, Delhi, Mumbai, and Banglore.
